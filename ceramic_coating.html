Our Coating Process

Preparation matters.

01

Inspection

We assess paint or wrap condition, surface defects, gloss levels and overall vehicle presentation before any work begins.

02

Detail & Decontamination

The vehicle is thoroughly detailed, chemically decontaminated and clay treated when needed to remove bonded contamination from the surface.

03

Paint Correction & Panel Prep

The paint is polished or corrected to improve clarity, gloss, and defect removal, then wiped down to remove polishing oils before coating.

04

Coating Application

Ceramic protection is carefully applied panel by panel using controlled methods, proper lighting and precise leveling.

05

Final Presentation

The finish is reviewed for gloss, consistency and overall appearance before delivery.
