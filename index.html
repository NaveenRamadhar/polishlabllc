FAQ

Everything you need to know before booking.

Answers to common questions about Polish Lab’s premium mobile detailing services in Orlando.

What is mobile detailing?
Mobile detailing is a professional cleaning, restoration and protection service performed at your home, office, hangar, marina or preferred location.

Polish Lab delivers studio-level results using deionized water, premium products and surface-safe techniques.
What areas do you service?
We service Orlando and surrounding areas including Winter Park, Windermere, Lake Nona, Dr. Phillips, Winter Garden and nearby communities.

For aircraft, marine, fleet or private client work, service areas can be confirmed by inquiry.
Do I need to provide water or power?
No. Polish Lab is fully mobile and self-sufficient.

Our van is equipped with deionized water, power and professional detailing equipment.
What’s the difference between detailing and a car wash?
A car wash focuses on speed and basic cleaning. Detailing is a more careful, precision-based process designed to clean, protect and preserve the vehicle.

Polish Lab uses hand-wash methods, premium products and safe techniques for luxury, exotic, wrapped, PPF and ceramic-coated vehicles.
How long does a detail take?
Most services take between 1 to 4 hours depending on the vehicle size, selected package and condition.

Larger vehicles, restoration work, aircraft, boats or fleet services may require additional time.
Do you detail aircraft, boats, and jetskis?
Yes. Polish Lab offers detailing for private aircraft, boats, jetskis and luxury marine surfaces.

Aircraft and marine services are handled by private inquiry so we can confirm the surface, location, access and scope of work.
How often should I get my vehicle detailed?
For luxury, exotic, and performance vehicles, we recommend maintenance detailing every 2 weeks.

This helps preserve gloss, reduce buildup and protect against Florida heat, UV exposure, water spots and environmental fallout.
Do you offer maintenance for returning clients?
Yes. Our Signature Maintenance Membership is designed for returning Polish Lab clients who want to keep their vehicle consistently clean and protected between deeper services.
