FAQ

Everything you need to know before booking with POLISH LAB.

What is mobile detailing?
Mobile auto detailing is a full-service, professional vehicle cleaning and restoration performed at your home, office, or preferred location.

At POLISH LAB, we specialize in luxury mobile car detailing in Orlando, FL, using DI (deionized) water, premium detailing products, and advanced techniques to safely clean, protect, and enhance your vehicle’s finish.

You get the convenience of on-site service with the quality and results of a high-end detailing studio.
What areas do you service in Orlando, FL?
We service Orlando and surrounding areas, including Winter Park, Windermere, Lake Nona, Dr. Phillips, Winter Garden, and nearby communities.

If you’re unsure whether your location is within our service area, feel free to contact us and we’ll be happy to confirm.
How often should I get my car detailed?
For luxury, exotic, or performance vehicles, we recommend a maintenance detail every 2–4 weeks to preserve a flawless finish and protect your paint from Florida’s heat, UV exposure, and environmental fallout.

Regular detailing helps prevent oxidation, water spots, and swirl marks, keeping gloss and protection levels consistent year-round.

Our Signature Maintenance Detailing Program offers discounted pricing and priority scheduling for returning clients.
What’s the difference between an auto detail and a car wash?
A traditional car wash focuses on speed and basic cleaning — often using harsh chemicals and improper techniques that can damage paint.

Auto detailing is a precision-based cleaning and restoration process. At POLISH LAB, we use hand washing, paint-safe methods, and ceramic-safe products to achieve a swirl-free, mirror-gloss finish.

Our detailing protects your vehicle’s paint, wheels, and interior at a level no standard car wash can match.
Do I need to provide water or power?
No — POLISH LAB is completely self-sufficient.

Our mobile detailing van is equipped with deionized water, a professional generator, and all necessary high-end equipment to complete your service anywhere in Orlando, FL.

You don’t need to provide anything — just access to the vehicle and the keys.
How long does a mobile detail appointment take?
Appointment times vary based on the service selected, vehicle size, and overall condition.

Most services take between 1 to 4 hours. Larger vehicles or deeper restoration services may require additional time.

Your booking confirmation will include an estimated service window.
How do I book an appointment with POLISH LAB?
Booking is easy.

Simply use our online booking system or contact us directly to schedule your mobile detailing service.

We’ll confirm your location, vehicle, and selected service. Our team arrives fully equipped and ready to deliver Orlando’s most premium mobile detailing experience.
Do you offer maintenance for returning clients?
Yes — the Signature Maintenance Detail is designed exclusively for returning POLISH LAB clients. It keeps your vehicle consistently clean and protected between deeper services, with discounted pricing and priority scheduling when available.
