Our Standard

We bring the lab to you.

Polish Lab was built on precision, consistency and premium-level detailing. We bring studio-quality results directly to your driveway, private garage, office, hangar, marina or preferred location.

Every service is performed using professional-grade equipment, deionized water systems, premium products and refined techniques designed to safely clean, protect and enhance your vehicle — whether it’s a daily driver, luxury SUV, exotic, performance build, wrapped vehicle, aircraft, boat or personal watercraft.
