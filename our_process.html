Our Process

At POLISH LAB, detailing is not rushed. It’s controlled, deliberate, and repeatable. Every step is designed to protect your vehicle while enhancing gloss, depth, and finish quality.

The POLISH LAB Exterior Process

We use deionized water, professional-grade equipment, and surface-safe techniques tailored for luxury, performance, ceramic-coated, and wrapped vehicles.

  1. Pre-Wash Decontamination

    A touchless pre-foam safely breaks down heavy grime, bugs, and environmental buildup before any physical contact is made.

  2. Safe Contact Wash

    pH-balanced foam and premium microfiber methods minimize swirl risk and preserve delicate finishes.

  3. DI Water Rinse

    A spot-free rinse removes residue and prepares the surface for protection.

  4. Blow-Dry & Crevice Detailing

    Filtered air removes water from tight areas to prevent streaking and spotting.

  5. Sealant Protection

    A professional-grade ceramic sealant enhances gloss, hydrophobic protection, and UV resistance.

  6. Final Inspection

    Every vehicle receives a final walk-around to ensure consistent, high-end results.

Zero shortcuts. Zero compromise.
